About

We needed it. So we built it.

01

The origin.

A decade of running lower-middle-market mandates on a fractured stack. Salesforce for sales teams, PitchBook for data, Outlook for correspondence. None of them understood a mandate.

The CRM stopped being useful. The group chat became the system of record. Associates spent hours copying information between systems that were never designed to talk to each other.

02

The build.

Retrofitting a CRM never works. The data model underneath was never designed for deal work. A mandate is not a sales opportunity. A buyer is not a lead. An IOI is not a quote.

We built from scratch with the right primitives. Not a CRM add-on. The operating system for a deal.

03. The philosophy

Invisible infrastructure.

AI in Atlas surfaces the right information at the right moment. It does not interrupt, explain itself, or require configuration. Nothing writes itself into the record without approval.

The best infrastructure is the kind you stop noticing.
