Our Story

Timeless charm,
thoughtfully preserved.

Ludington House is a lovingly restored 19th-century estate nestled in the rolling landscape of Connecticut. For over a century, these halls have welcomed families, friends, and communities for the moments they hold most dear.

Today, we host intimate ceremonies, grand receptions, private dinners, and corporate retreats — each tailored to the character of the house and the people inside it.
