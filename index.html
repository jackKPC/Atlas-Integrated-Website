The Platform

Built for how deals actually run.

Investment banking deal workflows live across spreadsheets, inboxes, and CRMs that were never designed for this work. The administrative coordination becomes its own job, growing with every active mandate.

Atlas replaces that fragmentation with a single record of the deal. Origination, execution, and delivery on one system.

Capabilities

Every part of running a deal, on one system.

From origination through delivery, the full lifecycle of a mandate captured in a single record.

AI in Atlas

Adopting Atlas means adopting AI for your bank.

The deal lifecycle has many different workflows. Atlas lets you spend time on the ones that matter: Interacting with clients and buyers. Let Atlas handle the rest.

We’ve deliberately injected AI where it fits naturally: Document Parsing, Market Research, Deal Tracking, and Data Enrichment. All wrapped in a modern UI displaying your data where you’d expect.

AI has arrived for investment banking. Don’t get left behind.

AI Deal Tracking

Suggests updates to the deal record and writes contact history notes from real activity.

You approve every change before it lands in the database.

Relationship Intelligence

Ranked relationships across your entire firm — who knows who, and how well.

Built from email history, prior deal participation, and meeting overlap. Scored automatically.

Imported Emails

Every email automatically appended to the right contact’s CRM record.

The full message history attached to every contact, with no copy-paste between systems.

Campaign Design & Management

Targeted buyer campaigns for every sell-side mandate — built around the right audience for each client.

Audience targeting, send sequencing, and performance metrics across active mandates side by side.

Atlas Mailman

Full outreach software built on Microsoft Graph API for maximum deliverability.

In-app reply tracking, opens, link clicks, and bounces — the full statistics suite in one place.

Contact Sourcing

One-click prioritized search across firm relationships, prior deals, and AI-assisted Apollo enrichment.

Known contacts surface first, then enriched candidates, all in a single pass.

Buyers List Generation

A four-phase buyer identification process that refines as the mandate advances.

Mandate intake, Pitchbook universe scoping, AI-driven web research, and fit ranking.

Native Pitchbook Integration

Type a buyer’s website. Pitchbook auto-enriches the entire CRM record.

Biographies, investment preferences, and the rest of the buyer record pulled in automatically.

Valuation Workshop

Integrated modeling environment with real-time scenario analysis.

Output-ready formatting for client deliverables, generated from the same dataset.

Virtual Data Room

Secure, permissioned document management with granular access logging.

Watermarking and buyer-side activity tracking surfaced directly in the deal record.

Reporting

On-demand report generation from live deal data.

Client-ready output, formatted for the audience and the stage of the engagement.

MCP Server

Easy integration with your enterprise AI subscriptions.

Get deal and CRM data from natural language queries in Claude, Perplexity, ChatGPT, and more.
