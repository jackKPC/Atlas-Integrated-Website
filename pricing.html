Pricing

Scoped to your firm, not a published grid.

Atlas pricing is structured to fit your firm — whether you are a two-person boutique or a 30-professional regional bank. We scope engagements based on team size, deal volume, and integration requirements.

A short conversation is enough for us to put a number on paper. We will ask about your active mandate count, team structure, and the systems you are replacing — and come back with a scope that fits.

Onboarding

Measured in days, not months.

Implementation is a short, well-defined sequence. Your existing workflow is enhanced, not replaced.

01

Scoping call

Day 1

A thirty-minute conversation on your team, active mandates, current stack, and the handful of integrations that matter. We leave with a concrete scope and a deployment date.

From your firm

One decision-maker on the call.

From Atlas

A scoped proposal within one business day.

02

Deployed to your infrastructure

Days 2 – 5

We port Atlas to your Microsoft infrastructure and stand up a dedicated instance inside your tenant. We partner exclusively with Azure’s OpenAI model deployments so that you own your data — it never goes to any third party. Deal and contact records are migrated from your current system, integrations are configured, and engagement history is staged for relationship intelligence.

From your firm

An Azure tenant with an Azure OpenAI deployment, an admin handoff for Microsoft Graph, a CRM export, and a named technical contact.

From Atlas

Atlas deployed inside your tenant, integrations connected, historical data loaded and validated — streamlining data compliance and AI token billing in one bill you already pay.

03

Team onboarding

Days 6 – 10

A guided session with the full team, followed by a week of hands-on support as real mandates move into the platform. A dedicated onboarding lead stays available through the first campaign.

From your firm

A one-hour team session and a named internal champion.

From Atlas

Live training, named onboarding lead, and direct support through your first full campaign.
