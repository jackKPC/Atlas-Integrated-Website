Product

Every part of running a mandate, on one system.

From origination through delivery, every capability your team needs to run a deal — inside a single record.

01

Origination

Finding the right buyers. Building the universe. Reaching out.

Contact Sourcing

Contact discovery across your firm’s relationships and Apollo in a single prioritized search. Known contacts first, fresh candidates next, deduplicated automatically.

In practice

Building an outreach list for a sell-side industrials mandate. Atlas pulls the senior operators your firm has previously engaged with at target firms and enriches each one with current titles, email addresses, and company context from Apollo — ready for outreach without leaving the engagement.

Buyers List Generation

A four-phase buyers list pipeline: Pitchbook search across financial and strategic acquirers, AI-driven web research, cross-engagement candidates, and ranking against your engagement history. Refined as the mandate advances.

In practice

Kick off a BL job on a new $150M industrials mandate. Twenty minutes later, Atlas returns a ranked universe of sponsors and strategics — auto-enriched with PitchBook data, marked up with the ones your firm has prior relationships with, and staged in the workspace for review.

Native Pitchbook Integration

Paste a buyer’s website. Atlas pulls the firm profile, investment thesis, recent transactions, and partner list from Pitchbook — all fielded into the CRM with zero manual re-entry.

In practice

Adding a new sponsor to the buyers list mid-mandate. Paste their URL. Atlas pulls the firm profile, investment thesis, recent deals, and key partners — all fielded correctly and ready for outreach in under a minute.

Email Campaigns

A campaign designer that pulls audiences directly from live deal data. Buyers who’ve already passed are excluded automatically. Contacts already updated don’t get a duplicate. Every send respects the current state of the deal — no misfires, no stepping on another thread.

In practice

Blast a teaser to 180 buyers on a Tuesday morning. Atlas handles send sequencing, Mailman throttles to preserve deliverability, and replies, opens, and clicks are logged against the deal record in real time — every signal in one place.

02

Execution

Running the mandate. Capturing activity. Keeping the record current without manual logging.

AI Deal Tracking

Atlas monitors your mailbox and extracts deal signals — contact history notes, milestone updates, campaign replies, new contacts — and queues them as suggestions. Every suggestion is an approve, edit, or dismiss decision, reviewed from Atlas or directly in the Outlook add-in.

In practice

A buyer replies with questions on the CIM. Atlas drafts a suggested contact history note and queues a milestone update from “Outreach” to “Review.” You tap approve in the Outlook sidebar and both land in the deal record.

CRM

Every mandate gets its own workspace with the buyers list, contacts, campaigns, inbox activity, documents, and milestones in one place. Buyers and contacts are canonical across the firm — one record per entity, no matter how many engagements touch it.

In practice

Open an engagement. See the full mandate state — the ranked buyer universe, last-touch timestamps per contact, the open campaigns, the unread suggestions queue, and the current milestone — in a single screen rather than five tools.

Relationship Intelligence

Who at your firm has worked with a given contact, on which prior deals, and in what capacity — surfaced automatically from your firm’s engagement history. Relationships compound across mandates instead of getting trapped in deal folders.

In practice

Prepping a pitch. Atlas surfaces that Partner B ran a sell-side for the prospect’s parent three years ago and an Associate has ongoing correspondence with the head of corp dev — ranked by frequency and deal involvement.

Buyer Scoring

Every buyer gets a ranked score against the engagement’s parameters — revenue, deal activity, fund stage, sector focus. The underlying rationale stays visible for review.

In practice

Refining the buyers list mid-process. Atlas scores every candidate against the mandate’s size and sector, and the top-ranked buyers surface their financial context — recent fund deployment, sector focus, and deal cadence — right in the list view.

03

Delivery

Client-ready documents, reports, and the data room — generated from the same engagement record.

Virtual Data Room

Documents live inside the mandate workspace, permissioned per buyer and watermarked on serve. Buyer-side access shows up as activity in the deal record — context for the next conversation.

In practice

A buyer accesses the financial model for the first time at 2am on a Thursday. Atlas logs the access, watermarks the served file, and surfaces the activity alongside the outreach history — context for the next conversation with that buyer.

One Click Reports

Engagement summaries, buyer timelines, and process updates produced on demand from live data. No manual assembly, no copy-paste into a deck.

In practice

Monday morning update for a client. One click produces the current state of the outreach — buyers contacted, responses received, meetings scheduled, and stage-appropriate next steps — as a formatted PDF, ready to share without a banker drafting a word.
